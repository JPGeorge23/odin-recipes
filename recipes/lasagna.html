Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious, classic lasagna recipe that is sure to please any crowd. It features a rich meat sauce and a creamy, cheesy filling that is layered between pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until beef is browned.
  3. Drain grease, and stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. To assemble, spread about 1 1/2 cups meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Arrange 6 noodles over meat sauce.
  9. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  10. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese.
  11. Repeat layers, and top with remaining meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  12. Cover with foil, and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes.
